About

Short Bio

My name is Andi Nika, and I am a PhD student in the Machine Teaching and Multi-agent Systems groups at MPI-SWS, Saarbrücken, Germany.

I completed my undergraduate degree in Mathematics at the University of Tirana in 2015. Following that, I took a year-long hiatus to explore my artistic passions, participating in The Voice of Albania and reaching the final nights. Afterward, I returned to academia, pursuing graduate degrees in Mathematics and Electrical and Electronics Engineering at Bilkent University, where I developed a strong interest in research in Machine Learning.
