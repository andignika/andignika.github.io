Hello!

My name is Andi Nika and I am a PhD student in the Machine Teaching and Multi-agent Systems groups at MPI-SWS, Saarbrücken, Germany.

I completed my undergraduate degree in Mathematics at the University of Tirana in 2015. Following that, I decided to take a year-long hiatus to delve into my artistic passions. During this time, I participated in The Voice of Albania, a prominent national musical contest, and made it to the final nights. Aferward, I decided to come back to the world of academia and pursue graduate degrees in Mathematics and Electrical and Electronics Engineering at Bilkent University. That is where I developed a strong interest for research in Machine Learning.

My Interests

My background is in mathematics and theoretical machine learning, and my main interests include:

-Reinforcement learning (RL) theory

-Robustness to adversarial attacks in RL

-Preference-based RL
